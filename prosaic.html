"Prosaic" refers both to prose in written language and to something boring. With the goal of an infographic for a chosen word, I illustrated statistics on boredom and language-related things. I envision this flyer hanging on a classroom wall, catching the eye of a bored student who reads that school is the top spot for boredom. I thought the flyer should have a silly and retro feel, and I wanted to debunk the common misconception that today's youth have lower attention spans and read less.
